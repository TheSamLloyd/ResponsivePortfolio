About Me

Hello! I'm a 24 year-old person from New Hampshire who does all kinds of stuff, including web development, statistics, and new wave music. I got my BA in 2016 from Ithaca College in Mathematics, and I'm studying for membership in the Society of Actuaries.

My favorite parts of working in development environments are the sudden moments of clarity and insight where you suddenly realize the most elegant way to solve a problem. I have a nasty habit of tweaking and fiddling until I feel I've found an "optimal" solution. This is great when I need to optimize code, but not great when working code is all that's necessary.

I recently adopted a kitten named Roswell who is the light of my life.
